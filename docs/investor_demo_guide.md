# 📊 Refer 2.0 Investor Demo Guide

This guide outlines the optimal sequence and talking points for demonstrating the Refer 2.0 platform to potential investors.

## 🎯 Demo Goal
Showcase how Refer 2.0 solves the "Trust & Transparency Gap" in real estate referrals using AI, Immutable Ledgers, and Embedded Finance.

---

## 🚀 The 12-Step Narrative Sequence

### **Phase 1: The Entry (Referrer Perspective)**
1.  **Splash & Identity**: Start at `/splash`. Explain the "Chain of Custody" and how every user (Referrer, Agent, Customer) is part of a verified ecosystem.
2.  **Referrer Hub**: Go to `/refer`. Show the **AI QR Generator**. Explain how "Proof of Introduction" is captured the moment a link is clicked.
3.  **Link Ledger**: Show the active links. Highlight the "Immutable Ledger" that prevents commission theft.

### **Phase 2: The Action (Customer Perspective)**
4.  **Customer Intake**: Show the high-fidelity intake form (Triggered via referral link). Explain how AI extracts intent (Budget, Timeline, Location).
5.  **AI Matchmaking**: Demonstrate the customer dashboard. Highlight the "AI Agent Match" score (98% match).
6.  **Embedded Finance**: Open the **Mortgage Calculator**. Show how we capture the customer *before* they go to a bank, owning the top of the funnel.

### **Phase 3: The Fulfillment (Agent Perspective)**
7.  **Priority Lead Card**: Switch to Agent Dashboard. Show the "Priority Lead" notification. Highlight the predictive "Conversion Probability".
8.  **AI Tenant Screening**: Open the **Screening Module**. Explain how we verify income and identity instantly, reducing agent workload by 80%.
9.  **Agent Collaboration**: Show the **Split Commission** tool. Explain how agents can co-work on deals with automated contract splits.

### **Phase 4: The Loop (Network & Growth)**
10. **Voice Assistant**: Open a chat. Use the **AI Voice Messenger**. Show how agents can update deal status via voice while on-site.
11. **Gamification**: Go to `/profile`. Show the **XP & Tiers**. Explain how this drives retention and data accuracy (Agents want that Platinum badge).
12. **Settlement Ledger**: Show the final payout screen. Explain the "Instant Settlement" loop via the Korg engine.
